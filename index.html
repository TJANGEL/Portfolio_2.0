About

Let me introduce myself.

Web developer with experience in front-end development and a passion for problem solving and a love for creating and design, currently learning full stack development. I not only want to use my design skills to make the application look beautiful, but I also want to work on the technically challenging facets of the application as well.

I have always had a love for technology and creating, which was missing in my most recent role as a real estate associate. I seek a role as a web developer at a company where I can continue to learn, grow and master my craft in this ever-evolving landscape of programming.
